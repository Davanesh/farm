PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana

This scheme provides income support to farmers by transferring Rs. 6,000 annually directly to their bank accounts in three installments.

Sub-Mission on Agroforestry (SMAF)

SMAF aims to encourage farmers to plant trees along with crops to enhance income and environmental benefits.

Fasal Bima Yojana

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana provides crop insurance against unforeseen events like natural calamities and pests.

Soil Health Card Scheme

The scheme provides soil health cards to farmers, offering information on soil nutrient status and recommendations for better farming practices.
