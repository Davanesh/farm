Crop health is vital for a good harvest. This section helps farmers identify, prevent, and treat pests and diseases effectively. 1.Crop-Specific Issues: Each crop is prone to specific diseases. For example: Paddy: Blast disease (fungal infection) and stem borer pests. Sugarcane: Red rot disease and top shoot borer. Provides identification techniques, such as recognizing discoloration, wilting, or pest trails. 2.Natural Remedies: Guides on preparing eco-friendly solutions like neem oil sprays, chili-garlic extracts, and fermented cow urine for pest control. Promotes Integrated Pest Management (IPM), which combines natural and minimal chemical solutions. 3.Chemical Solutions: Safe use of pesticides, including selecting the right product, mixing ratios, and application timing. Instructions on adhering to government-approved guidelines to avoid overuse or residue problems. 4.Prevention Tips: Field sanitation, crop rotation, and using resistant seed varieties to reduce pest and disease occurrences.
