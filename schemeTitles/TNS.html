Tamil Nadu Mission on Sustainable Dryland Agriculture (TNAIM-SDA): Designed for rainfed areas, this scheme focuses on increasing the productivity of dryland crops. Key Benefits: Assistance for pulses, millets, and oilseed cultivation. Unique Feature: Promotes agroforestry and intercropping in water-scarce regions. Agro-Processing and Marketing Scheme: Encourages the establishment of small-scale agro-processing units for value addition to farm produce. Key Benefits: Subsidies for setting up cold storage, packaging units, and processing plants. Unique Feature: Helps farmers earn more by reducing post-harvest losses and improving product quality. Nadum Mavatta Pasumai Kuzhandhaigal Project: A unique program aimed at creating green fodder banks for livestock farmers. Key Benefits: Ensures year-round fodder availability for cattle and increases milk production. Unique Feature: Focuses on improving animal husbandry alongside crop farming. Special Livestock Protection Scheme: Offers free veterinary camps, vaccination drives, and medicines for livestock. Key Benefits: Enhances livestock health and productivity. Unique Feature: Available even to small and marginal farmers with limited livestock. Farm Pond Scheme: Encourages farmers to construct farm ponds for rainwater harvesting. Key Benefits: Subsidies for pond construction and lining materials. Unique Feature: Focuses on water conservation in drought-prone areas.
